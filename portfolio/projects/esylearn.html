Overview

EsyLearn allows you to easily get fast, spoken answers from ChatGPT. Just ask a question and receive a verbal answer within seconds.

The project was inspired by growing skepticism around AI's role in education. We viewed ChatGPT not as a hurdle, but as a tool to aid educational processes.

Goals

  • Showcase how AI can help learning, addressing fears of ChatGPT being used to cheat.
  • Assist individuals who struggle with reading or writing through the audio function.

APIs used

  • Flask
  • SpeechRecognition
  • Recorder.js
  • OpenAI API
